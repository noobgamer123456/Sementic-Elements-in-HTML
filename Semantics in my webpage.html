About Valorant

Valorant is a 5v5 tactical shooter game developed by Riot Games. Released in 2020, it quickly gained popularity for its unique blend of strategic gameplay and diverse cast of agents, each with their own abilities. Players must work together to plant or defuse the Spike while mastering their agent's abilities and aiming precision. The game emphasizes teamwork, strategy, and mechanical skill, making it one of the most competitive shooters today.

Did you know that Valorant features agents from various backgrounds, each representing a different country? For example, Jett hails from South Korea, while Phoenix is from the UK. Each agent brings a distinct playstyle, ranging from duelists like Reyna to strategists like Viper. Choose wisely to complement your team!
